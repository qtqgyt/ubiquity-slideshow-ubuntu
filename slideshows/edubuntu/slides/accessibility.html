Activate Accessibility

  • We want to make computers work for everyone, whatever your physical circumstances. So, we provide tools that make Edubuntu one of the most accessible operating systems around.
  • You can get at these tools in one place: the Accessibility Preferences, inside the System Settings. From there, you can turn on helpful tools like the Screen Reader, to speak text on the screen, or Hover Click to press mouse buttons automatically.
  • Remember to check out the Appearance Settings, too. You can choose between dark and light styles, change the background, and even change the accent color.
